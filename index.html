I've spent my life learning how to do things. From veterinary medicine to fire science and many things in between, I've learned how to do a lot of things. This has made me, for the most part, a jack-of-many-trades, but master of none.


Everything changed when I discovered web development.


Designing and coding websites by hand, and writing the code that makes them work behind the scenes, is exhilarating. It is the art of making beautiful things from ones and zeroes. It is one of the purest forms of "creatio ex nihilo," creation from nothing. And it is my passion.


Having spent my life as a jack-of-many-trades, I am now driven to become a master web developer. I still have a great deal to learn, but I seek every opportunity to learn what I can, to expand my skillset, and to be the best web developer, the best creator, I can be.


I am a husband, a father, and an artist. I am a full-stack web developer.
